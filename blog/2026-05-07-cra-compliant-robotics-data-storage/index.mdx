## The CRA deadline every robotics operator should prepare for

The CRA (Regulation (EU) 2024/2847) entered into force on 10 December 2024 with major milestones approaching:

- **11 September 2026**: mandatory reporting of actively exploited vulnerabilities and severe incidents
- **11 December 2027**: full compliance obligations, including secure-by-design expectations, lifecycle support, technical documentation, and CE-related evidence

For AMRs, cobots, autonomous systems, and ROS 2 platforms, the pressure is higher because these systems generate large multimodal streams (camera, LiDAR, IMU, logs, ROS bags) under constrained edge hardware and unreliable connectivity.

## Why common storage setups struggle with CRA in production

Many teams still rely on combinations of rosbag2, generic object stores (MinIO/S3), and TSDBs like InfluxDB or TimescaleDB. While workable for parts of the stack, they usually require extensive custom glue for:

- complete auditability
- selective data minimization
- robust integrity verification
- compliance-ready reporting evidence

That added complexity increases both delivery cost and the risk of documentation gaps during conformity assessments.
